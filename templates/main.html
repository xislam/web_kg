WHAT SHOULD I DO

Jailoo excursion

A Jailoo excursion offers the full experience of Kyrgyz nomadic culture. Nights in the pristine countryside are incredibly peaceful and tranquil.

Kumy's Cure

Kumys is a result of the fermentation of mare's milk. It is obtained from our pastures and is so healthy that doctors recommend it in the treatment of diseases.

Eat Drink

The traditional Kyrgyz cuisine is characterized by meat and dairy products from species-appropriate husbandry with a natural appearance and excellent taste.

eagle hunt

Kyrgyzstan is one of the few places in the world where hawking with raptors is still practiced. A special experience just by watching.

Hiking & Tracking

Kyrgyzstan is an outdoor enthusiast's paradise. It offers an unbelievable number of possibilities for alpine mountaineering or trekking from easy to extreme.

Rockclimbing

About 95% of Kyrgyzstan's territory is covered by mountain ranges, the Tien Shan and the Pamir. They form the largest contiguous mountain system in the world.

To go biking

The most popular exploration method is cycling. Lovers of extreme sports and long mountain bike tours will appreciate the potential.

Jeep and engine

Approximately 70% of roads in Kyrgyzstan are unpaved. This makes the country an ideal place for off-road experiences with our jeep and motorbike tours.

horseback riding tours

Riding trails lead through landscapes with untouched mountain lakes, beautiful spruce forests and alpine meadows in high mountain valleys.
